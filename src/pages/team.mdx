## AusTrakka Co-Founder

### Dr Anders Gonçalves da Silva
**Co-founder & Lead Developer**

Dr Anders Gonçalves da Silva co-founded AusTrakka alongside Prof. Torsten Seemann and played a pivotal role in shaping the platform into a nationally significant public health genomics resource. A highly skilled computational evolutionary biologist, he brought expertise in population genetics, bioinformatics, and data analysis to AusTrakka.

> Dr Gonçalves da Silva passed away in 2023, but his contributions remain foundational to the program's ongoing success.
